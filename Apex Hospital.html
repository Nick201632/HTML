Health Checkup

Apex Hospital offers comprehensive health checkup packages designed to help detect health conditions early and promote a healthier lifestyle. Our experienced medical team uses advanced diagnostic technology to provide accurate results and personalized healthcare recommendations.

Our Health Checkup Packages

Package Includes
Basic Health Checkup Blood Pressure, Blood Sugar, BMI, Doctor Consultation
Standard Health Checkup Basic Package + ECG, Blood Tests, Urine Test
Advanced Health Checkup Standard Package + X-Ray, Ultrasound, Liver & Kidney Function Tests
Executive Health Checkup Full Body Checkup, ECG, Chest X-Ray, Eye Test, Dental Checkup, Specialist Consultation

Benefits of Regular Health Checkups

  • Early detection of health conditions
  • Regular monitoring of your overall health
  • Prevention of future illnesses
  • Personalized health and lifestyle advice
  • Peace of mind through routine screenings

Before Your Health Checkup

  • Fast for 8–10 hours if instructed.
  • Drink water unless advised otherwise.
  • Inform your doctor about any medications.
  • Bring previous medical records if available.
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your appointment.
