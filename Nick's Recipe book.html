  1. Chicken Biryani

Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour 50 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Basmati Rice 3 Cups
Chicken 1 kg
Yogurt 1 Cup
Biryani Masala 2 Spoons
Onions 3
Tomatoes 2
Ginger-Garlic paste 2 spoons
Oil 4 spoons
Salt For taste
Coriander Leaves A handful
Mint leaves A handful

  • Raita
  • Chicken 65

Cooking Information

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 50 minutes

About Chicken Biryani

  • Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and aromatic spices. It is one of the most popular dishes in India and is enjoyed on special occasions and family gatherings.
  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook them until soft, then add the marinated chicken.
  5. Cook the chicken until it is tender and fully cooked.
  6. Add the soaked rice and enough water, then cook until the rice is soft and fluffy
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander or mint leaves and serve hot.
